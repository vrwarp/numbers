# Vocabulary & aliases

- "the retreat" or "church camp" usually means the all-church **Summer Retreat**.
- "Footprint" / "youth group" is the youth fellowship; their fall trip is the **Youth Retreat**.
- English small groups: Ember, Grace, Joshua (Joshua is the Mandarin fellowship).
- "Friday night program" refers to the children's Friday Night Program, not youth.

# Recurring events

- Summer Retreat — early July, at Redwood Camp.
- Youth Retreat — usually October.
- VBS — one week in June.
- Christmas Celebration — churchwide outreach event, mid-December.

# Labeling rules

- Food/snack purchases default to **Luncheon Catering** unless the description
  ties them to a named event or fellowship.
- Anything mentioning "kids"/"children" belongs to a Children/Education
  category, not the youth fellowship.
- Cleaning and paper goods for the building are Janitorial & Cleaning, not
  Office Supplies.
